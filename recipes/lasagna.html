This is the Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of noodles, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese.

It is hearty, comforting, and perfect for sharing, with each slice holding together the savory layers of sauce, pasta, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. Brown the ground beef with onion and garlic, drain excess fat, and stir in marinara sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta with the egg, a pinch of salt, and some Parmesan.
  5. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in the dish, repeating until full.
  6. Finish with sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake 10–15 minutes more.
  8. Rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
